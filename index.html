Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Carleton University working under the supervision of Prof. Shichao Liu. My research focuses on cyber-physical power systems. Previously, I held postdoctoral positions at POSTECH with Prof. Poogyeon Park and at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University with Prof. Nan Guan.


欢迎来到我的主页！
