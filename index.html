Yibo Wang | 王毅博

 

Welcome to my homepage!

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at Carleton University, Canada, working in the field of fuzzy systems. Previously, I held postdoctoral and research associate positions at POSTECH, South Korea and The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. My research interests include time-delay systems, robust control, and neural networks.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Yanshan University, China, under the supervision of Prof. Changchun Hua. During my Ph.D., I also studied as an exchange student at POSTECH, Korea.
